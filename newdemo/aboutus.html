About Us

bikeShed was founded in 2015 with the goal to make two wheeler vehicle repair and maintenance affordable, convenient, and transparent. We startedbikeShed because we believed that the auto repair industry was broken.
Dealing with bike problems is not only a time-consuming and frustrating experience, it is often expensive. Bike owners often lack visibility regarding the quality of mechanics or fairness of price. At the same time, the mechanic sheds that put in the hard work fixing bikes make very little money (less than 20% of what consumers pay at other dealers), and rarely get the recognition they deserve.

Building a better experience for bike owners

Life’s too short to spend it at a repair shop. We offer services at the convenience of your home or office, 7 days a week, and save you up to 30% on bike repair and maintenance.
We are on a mission to build the most convenient and trust service. Total trust comes from total transparency.
All the pricing is calculated based on industry standard labor times to perform services. This ensures that bike owners are paying a fair price without the need to negotiate or shop around. We back the services provided in our platform with a warranty.

Empowering mechanics to live a better life

By connecting mechanics and local bike sheds directly with bike owners in need of repair services, we are empowering these hard-working mechanics to control their quality of life. They are able to choose their work rates, when they want to work (including evenings and weekends), and the type ofbikes and services they want to work on.
By working directly with bikeShed, they now have an opportunity to earn a better living. We provide them access to customers, insurance, and high-quality parts. Bike sheds can focus on what they love—working on bikes—and we take care of the rest.
